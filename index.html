  • Accessing Network Shared Drives & Folders

Shared drives and folders are still accessible, and you will find these in either the PUBLIC DRIVE or the STAFF DRIVE

all STAFF can see folders in STAFF DRIVE, but only the relevant users will be able to open some folders - for example only members of the Science Department will be able to open the Science folder, even though all users will be able to see that there is a Science folder.
If you can't access a folder that you were expecting to be able to access please let IT support know by email (support@camdensitss.org.uk)
